Lost in the Middle

How document position affects LLM accuracy

The "U-Shaped" Discovery

Models are best at using information at the beginning (Primacy Bias) or the end (Recency Bias) of a context. Performance drops significantly—sometimes by over 20%—when the answer is buried in the middle[cite: 15, 198].

22.0%

Compared to "First Position" accuracy[cite: 198].

Key takeaway: Even "long-context" models like GPT-3.5-Turbo-16k show this curve[cite: 15, 202]. Adding more context can actually decrease accuracy[cite: 67].
