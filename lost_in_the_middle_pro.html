Lost in the Middle

An interactive visualization of how language models struggle to use information located in the middle of long input contexts, creating a distinctive "U-shaped" performance curve.

Primacy Bias

Models demonstrate high accuracy when the relevant information is placed at the very beginning of the prompt.

The "Middle" Slump

Accuracy degrades significantly when models must access and use information buried in the middle of the input text.

Recency Bias

Performance partially rebounds when the target information occurs at the very end of the input context.
